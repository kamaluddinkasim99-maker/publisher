About the Journal

Journal of Ecology and Environmental Management

Journal of Ecology and Environmental Management adalah jurnal ilmiah yang berfokus pada kajian ekologi, pengelolaan lingkungan, konservasi sumber daya alam, perubahan iklim, tata kelola lingkungan, pencemaran, wilayah pesisir, laut, serta pembangunan berkelanjutan.

Jurnal ini menerima artikel hasil penelitian, review article, short communication, dan policy brief yang memberikan kontribusi terhadap pengembangan ilmu pengetahuan, praktik pengelolaan lingkungan, serta pengambilan kebijakan berbasis bukti.

Editorial Board

KK

Editor in Chief

Kamaluddin Kasim, M.Sc.

Ph.D in Earth Science and Technology, Institut Teknologi Bandung

Kamaluddin Kasim, M.Sc. is a fisheries researcher and stock assessment expert with extensive experience in marine and fisheries research, sustainable fisheries management, and evidence-based policy support. He has contributed to national and regional projects on fish stock assessment, catch quota allocation, demersal fisheries, mud crab, eel fisheries, red snapper fisheries in the Arafura Sea, and fisheries policy studies in Indonesia.

His academic background includes an M.Sc. from the School of Aquatic and Fishery Sciences, University of Washington, USA, and ongoing Ph.D. study in Earth Science and Technology at Institut Teknologi Bandung. His selected publications cover blue economy strategy, environmental sustainability, fishing violation hotspots, catch quota allocation, cantrang fisheries, and fisheries management policy.
