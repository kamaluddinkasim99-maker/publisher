Submit Your Manuscript

Prepare your manuscript carefully and complete the submission information below. The editorial team will conduct an initial screening before peer review.

Before Submission

Author Guidelines

Authors are required to ensure that the submitted manuscript is original, has not been published elsewhere, and is not currently under consideration by another journal. The manuscript should follow the scope, formatting requirements, and publication ethics of the Journal of Ecology and Environmental Management (JEEM).

01

Originality

The manuscript must be original, free from plagiarism, and properly cited.

02

Scope Relevance

The topic should relate to ecology, environmental management, coastal and marine studies, conservation, climate change, or sustainability.

03

Manuscript Format

Authors should follow the journal template and provide complete author and manuscript information.

04

Ethics Statement

Authors must comply with research ethics, publication ethics, and authorship responsibility.

Editorial Workflow

Submission Process

1

Manuscript Metadata Submission

Authors complete the online metadata form through the JEEM website. This information is submitted to the editorial office through Formspree.

2

Manuscript File Upload

Authors upload the manuscript file and supplementary documents through the official JEEM Google Form. Files will be stored in Google Drive.

3

Editorial Screening

The editorial office checks the scope, formatting, completeness, authorship information, and publication ethics compliance.

4

Peer Review

Manuscripts that pass editorial screening will be reviewed by qualified reviewers using a blind review process.

5

Revision, Decision, and Publication

Authors may be requested to revise the manuscript based on reviewer and editor comments before final decision and publication.
