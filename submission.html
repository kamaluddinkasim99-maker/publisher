Author Center

Submit Your Manuscript

Prepare your manuscript carefully and complete the submission information below. The editorial team will conduct an initial screening before peer review.

Before Submission

Author Guidelines

Authors are required to ensure that the submitted manuscript is original, has not been published elsewhere, and is not currently under consideration by another journal. The manuscript should follow the journal scope and formatting requirements.

01

Originality

The manuscript must be original and free from plagiarism.

02

Scope Relevance

The topic should relate to ecology, environment, coastal studies, or sustainability.

03

Manuscript Format

Use the journal template and follow the prescribed structure.

04

Ethics Statement

Authors must comply with research and publication ethics.

Editorial Workflow

Submission Process

1

Manuscript Submission

Author completes the submission form and uploads manuscript files.

2

Editorial Screening

The editorial office checks scope, formatting, completeness, and ethics compliance.

3

Peer Review

The manuscript is reviewed by qualified reviewers using a blind review process.

4

Revision and Decision

Authors revise the manuscript based on reviewer and editor comments.

5

Publication

Accepted manuscripts are copyedited, formatted, and published in the journal issue.
